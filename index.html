The Journey

Sound Luminescence is rooted in the observable, measurable energies of the Universe — from galactic clusters down to the tiniest sub-atomic particles. These natural energies constantly interact with every living creature and inanimate object, being reflected, refracted, or allowed to pass through partially or completely.

Everyone and everything emits their own combination of measurable energies: sonic (sound), light (colors including invisible infrared and ultraviolet), and other forms of radiation. All too often, this signature of emissions contains the phenomena of discord.

For example, if your body emits sound waves corresponding with the musical notes C and D, there is discord; whereas with C and E there is harmony. Likewise, on the 49th octave — the color spectrum with its own 7 'notes' — the colors Red and Orange clash, but Red and Yellow are complementary or harmonic.

“With this in mind, one can say that the musical note middle C would be discordant with the color orange. As with musical notes, there exist half-notes in the color spectrum. Each octave throughout the electromagnetic spectrum has 7 notes — a total of 12 half-notes. All of this can be calculated mathematically to determine harmony or discord.”

By taking careful measurements of a person's emissions while in a meditative state, we can determine any and all discordant emanation. Because the output can vary based on activity, health, or mood, we establish a baseline measurement in deep meditation.

With a baseline, we determine what is 'off', and through therapy we gently tune it toward the ideal frequency using intense light and/or sound vibrations. To take it a step further, we address the issue of what Key we're playing in. Different keys can sound sad, eerie, happy, etc. For an overall life experience, most of us would want a happy baseline — we strive to re-tune everything to the key of E (E, G-sharp, B) and the color equivalent (Yellow, Purple, Violet).

Simultaneously, we address body and mind rhythms, ensuring everything is synchronized. Having accomplished internal harmony is only the beginning. We must also consider the effects and influences of those close to us. If their 'music' does not resonate with our own, it is advisable to bring them in so as to create a fully symphonic family.

The Soul

The soul is the essence of your consciousness, identity, and awareness — the mind in its fullest expression.

It is the sum of your thoughts, character, dreams, and desires. The soul is not merely something you have; it is what you are. It radiates through your presence, shaping every interaction and every moment of your life.

Yet, the soul is almost always kept secluded deep within — and that is a shame. One's soul should radiate beyond the flesh to be seen and felt by all. The corporeal body's purpose is to keep the brain (and thus the soul) alive and serve its needs and wants.

With the body properly shrouded within the soul, one loses the suppositious need to adorn the carnal body. With the soul properly exposed, one will want to embellish it instead.

It is not without cause that the soul is usually kept hidden away. We all have plenty of blemishes and perhaps festering sores within, making us ashamed of our own souls. However, it is only by bringing the soul to the surface — along with its baggage — that we can fix it and make it beautiful once more. Can there be anything more beautiful than the soul of an innocent young child? Let us make the return to that state our goal.

Anyone who is determined to be evil and/or intentionally causes harm to others will not be willing to have his or her soul exposed. Luckily, actions speak volumes, so we may avoid them if we can maintain the clarity of mind to perceive them.

*An exposed soul is not literally visible or tangible, yet its radiance can tend to blind the observer to physical blemishes, disfigurement, or generally unpicturesqueness of the owner.
